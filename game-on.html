What you'll need

Dice

Dice come in a variety of types; D4, D6, D8, D10, D12, D20 and more!

Pencil

Pencils are more useful then pens because character sheets get altered, sometimes quite a bit.

Paper

Having a bit of blank paper helps for keeping notes or making maps.

Mobile, Tablet or Computer

With any computer, you can also access apps for character sheets, dice and the Discord Channel.


Players

Players are the primary characters in the game, the heroes or the villains. Play anything from Wizards to Ninjas, Space Rangers to Super Heroes.

Games Master

Think of the Games Master as the director of a movie; they develop the story, play all the other characters and provide any additional material required.
